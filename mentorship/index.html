Mentorship

Clarity is kindness.

Most mentorship questions look like 'what should I do?' The better ones turn into 'what do I actually want?' My role is to help you ask the better one.

I do this work because I've benefited from it myself, and because I notice that the people who change my life rarely give me answers — they give me a sharper version of my own question. I try to be that for the people who reach out.

How it works

  • Free for the first conversation. An honest 30-minute call. We figure out together if I'm the right person.
  • Async by default. Most of the work happens in writing and voice notes. Calls are for inflection points.
  • I take a small number of mentees at a time. Quality over quantity. If I can't help right now, I will say so.
  • No pricing on this page yet. I'm still figuring out what's fair. For now, write to me — most ongoing mentorship is currently no-fee or pay-what-feels-right.
