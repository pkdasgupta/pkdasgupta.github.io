Frameworks

Three frameworks.

These are not original. They are the distilled, much-simplified versions of ideas I have absorbed over twenty years from mentors, books, and a lot of evening walks. They are useful because I have used them; they are honest because I keep noticing where they fail.

01

The Life Equation

Experience, faithfully attended to, becomes Learning. Learning, slowly compounded, becomes Clarity. Clarity, used well, becomes a freer life and — eventually — a useful one.

The Three Pillars
